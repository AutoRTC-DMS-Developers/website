Scientific Publications State of The Art Review

The "state-of-the-art review" is an examination and summary of the current best practices, policies, techniques, and technologies in road traffic crashes management. The paper covers RTCs (Road Traffic Crashes) existing reporting tools / applications, contributing research work, Overview of road crash data management systems and discussion around trends, limitations of existing systems and recommendations.
