Evidence-based Road Safety: an Automatic Road Traffic Crashes Data Management System

Road traffic crashes and associated road traffic injuries cause the death of approximately 1.35 million people per year.

Road traffic crashes are under-reported, especially in low-income countries in which around 80% of road crash deaths are not recorded.

Project Overview

Summary

The AutoRTC-DMS project aims at designing and bringing to the market innovative tools and applications to record, analyze, and share road traffic crashes information in Rwanda. The main deliverables of the AutoRTC-DMS will include: cross-platform, multi-devices mobile applications for road crashes recording and reporting, a repository of road traffic crashes, a web-based dashboard displaying road traffic crashes statistics, and machine learning algorithms for a deeper analysis on road traffic crashes.
