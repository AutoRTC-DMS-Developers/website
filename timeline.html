Project Implementation Approach
The project implementation is divided into 5 work packages (WP). A work package represents a defined deliverable or a specific project phase.
WP 1 — Road Traffic Crashes Reporting Requirement Analysis and Specification

In this work package, the team conducted a broad analysis of the state of the art to reach a common understanding of road traffic crashes management field in Rwanda. Guided by the industrial partner, the team produced the state of art of raod crashes, a specification of the business, functional and nonfunctional requirements of the AutoRTC-DMS.

Timeline: M1 - M3

Status: COMPLETED

Deliverables: State of the art (SOTA), Requirement specification, Architecture, Functional and business requirements.

View report

WP 2 — Road Traffic Crashes Reporter, Repository and Dashboard

In this work package, the team will work on describing the structure of the system to be implemented, data models, interfaces between system components, database, API and algorithms, all of which they will then convert the specification into an executable system.

Timeline: M4 - M24

Status: IN PROGRESS

Deliverables: Application workflow and wireframes, Database design - ERD, Database, AutoRTC-DMS API, Mobile applications (Android, iOS and USSD) and AutoRTC-DMS dashboard

View report

WP 3 — Road Traffic Crashes Analytics Framework

In this work package, the team will design and implement an analytic framework that will uncover patterns and correlations to assist the system stakeholders in taking data-driven decisions in addressing Road Traffic Crashes and Incidents.

Timeline: M13 - M24

Status: NOT STARTED

Deliverables: RTCs analytics charts and Model identification, Road Traffic Crashes Analytics and Updated dashboard

View report

WP 4 — Communication, Dissemination and Outreach

In this work package, the team will focus on raising awareness of AutoRTC-DMS to different stakeholders and providing training. Different communication media will be considered depending on the target audience, and feedback will be collected in form of reports.

Timeline: M1 - M36

Status: ONGOING

Deliverables: Communication, Dissemination and Outreach plan, Communication tools setup, Project reports & Scientific publication, Workshop (Year 1): Project presentation and stakeholder engagement, Workshop (Year 2): Product presentation / training

View report

WP 5 — Project Management and Coordination

This work package will focus on team management, quality management, Contracts and reporting. It will span the whole project lifecycle, and it will mostly be based on reporting and financing.

Timeline: M1 - M36

Status: ONGOING

Deliverables: Quality Management Plan, Quality Reports, Project Management Plan Updates, Training Reports, Commercial agreements, Financial Reports and Project Status Reports, Signed Contracts and Operational Reports
