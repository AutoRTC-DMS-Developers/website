Evidence-based Road Safety: an Automatic Road Traffic Crashes Data Management System

Home > Project timeline

The project is subdivided into 5 main Work Packages (WP)

WP1

3 months

RTCs Reporting Requirement Analysis and Specification

Broad analysis of the state of the art to reach a common understanding of the road traffic crashes management field.

Deliverables

  • State of the art (SOTA) and Requirement specification
  • Architecture, functional and business requirements

WP2

4 - 24 months

RTCs Reporter, Repository & Dashboard

Description of the structure of the system to be implemented, data models, interfaces between system components, database, API and algorithms, all of which will then convert the specification into an executable system.

Deliverables

  • Application workflow & wireframes
  • Database design - ERD
  • Database
  • AutoRTC-DMS API
  • Android, iOS & USSD applications
  • AutoRTC-DMS Dashboard

WP3

13 - 24 months

RTCs Anayltics Framework

Design and implement an analytic framework that will uncover patterns and correlations to assist the system stakeholders in taking data driven decisions in addressing RTCs and RTIs.

Deliverables

  • RTCs Analytics Charts & Model Identification
  • RTCs Analytics & Improved Dashboard

WP4

1 - 36 months

Communication, Dissemination & Outreach

Raising awareness of AutoRTC-DMS to different stakeholders and providing training.

Deliverables

  • Communication, Dissemination & Outreach plan
  • Communication tools setup
  • Project reports & Scientific publication
  • Workshop (Year 1): Project presentation & stakeholder engagement
  • Workshop (Year 2): Product presentation / training

WP5

1 - 36 months

Project Management & Coordination

Team management, quality management, Contracts and reporting spanning the whole project lifecycle

Deliverables

  • Quality Management Plan, Quality Reports
  • Project Management Plan Updates
  • Training Reports
  • Commercial agreements, Financial Reports & Project Status Reports
  • Signed Contracts & Operational Reports
