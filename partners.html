National Council for Science and Technology

Grant Award

The National Council for Science and Technology (NCST) has the mandate to enable Rwanda to become a country based on science, technology and innovation as tools for promoting social welfare and sustainable socio economic development of the country and competitiveness on the market

Visit Website

Kigali Collaborative Research Centre

Project Coordinator

Established in 2016, Kigali Collaborative Research Centre (KCRC) aims to be a leading research organization in Africa dedicated to establishing and facilitating high standard and diverse research and innovations. The centre provides to an enabling environment for collaborative research and innovations in Africa.
Research areas include but not limited to ICT, energy systems, control systems, data science, artificial intelligence, transportation, water, sanitation and climate change.

Visit Website

Carnegie Mellon University Africa

Provide R&D engineers specialized in Software Engineering, Data science, User Interaction Design, Database Management Systems, and Network Optimization.

Carnegie Mellon University Africa (CMU-Africa) was established in 2011 from the partnership between Carnegie Mellon University and The Government of Rwanda. Its vision is to educate and empower the next generation of African leaders and innovators by delivering a world class educational experience.

African Center of Excellence in Data Science

Provide R&D engineers specialized in Data Science, Technical Support, Space & Infrastructure required for the successful completion of the project.

The African Center of Excellence in Data Science (ACE-DS) is one of the 24 Eastern and Southern Africa Higher Educations Centers of Excellence in the World Bank's ACE Ⅱ Project.
The ACE-DS is based at the University of Rwanda in the school of Business and Economics
